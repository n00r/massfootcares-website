Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do Diabetics need special Footwares?

    Poorly controlled blood sugar and long standing diabetes can damage many parts of the body, including the nerves and vessels that go to the feet. Because of this, people with diabetes have an increased risk of developing foot problems. Wearing specially designed footwares can help reduce risk and promote healthy circulation in your feet.

    These kind of special Diabetics chapels or diabetics Footwares in Tirunelveli are provided by Mass Footcares.

  • What are the benefits of diabetic Footwares?

    Prevent calluses and promote blood circulation as it reduces pressure points and distribute pressure evenly.

    Advanced level of comfort as Mass footcares footwear is tailor made to your foot specifications of size and fitting.

    Pain relief as it is designed keeping your foot dynamics in mind.

    Prevent skin breakdown (foot ulcers) Mass Footcares are hypo allergic and have soft uppers with anti fungal polycot ton lining.
